License Templates

Manage and deploy standardized smart-contract licenses for your registered Intellectual Property on the Stellar network.

storefront
Standard

Commercial Use v1

Grants full rights for monetization and commercial deployment of the associated IP. Suitable for retail products and mass media distribution.

public
Creative Commons

Non-Commercial CC

Allows free use and distribution for non-profit purposes. Requires strict attribution to the original creator on-chain.

sync_alt
Critical

Exclusive Transfer

Executes a full, immutable transfer of IP ownership to the licensee. Irrevocable once signed on the Soroban network.

hourglass_empty
Time-Bound

30-Day Evaluation

A smart contract that automatically expires 30 days after activation. Useful for software trials or temporary exhibition rights.

alt_route
Flexible

Derivative Works

Permits the licensee to remix, tweak, and build upon the original work. Any new works must acknowledge the original IP.

corporate_fare
Enterprise

Internal Corporate Use

Restricts usage strictly to within a specified corporate entity. Explicitly prohibits external sub-licensing or public distribution.
